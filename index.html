Urban Flood Risk Assessment
Harris County (Houston), TX·Hydrology & Risk Scoring·Completed

Multi-layer flood vulnerability analysis for one of the most flood-prone metro areas in the US. Combines SRTM elevation data (30m resolution), the FEMA National Flood Hazard Layer, and US Census population data to generate a composite 0–1 vulnerability index per census tract. Final outputs validated against documented Hurricane Harvey (2017) inundation extents, confirming high-risk cluster alignment at the Buffalo Bayou corridor, Addicks/Barker reservoir spillways, and Houston Ship Channel industrial zones.

Land Use / Land Cover Change Detection
Binghamton, NY Metro Area·Multi-temporal Raster Analysis·In Progress

Comparing NLCD (National Land Cover Database) classifications from 2011 and 2021 to quantify urban sprawl and green space loss across the Broome County metro area. A raster change detection matrix identifies class-to-class transitions (e.g., forest → developed, wetland → cultivated), producing area statistics and a choropleth map highlighting conversion hotspots over the decade.

Transit Accessibility & Equity Analysis
Broome County, NY·Network Analysis & Equity Mapping·In Progress

Walking catchment areas generated from GTFS bus stop coordinates using QNEAT3 service area analysis, then intersected with ACS low-income population data to identify transit deserts — areas with high need and poor coverage. Outputs a bivariate choropleth surfacing equity gaps to support local transit planning decisions.
